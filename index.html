ABOUT ME

My name is Rick Flynn, and over the past 15 years I've coached scores of students with their college entrance and scholarship statements.

I'll help you find and develop those things you've done that make you shine, and show you how to shape them into memorable prose.

Using a mix of discussion, targeted tasks, editing and polishing, I'll guide you from your first brainstorm to the final, polished product.

CONSULTATION

We'll start with brain storming, mind-mapping, talking and probing to figure out those things you've done which define your character. I love listening to people's stories and have great ears for the ones that will work.

WRITING

We'll start with a free-flowing first draft, like panning for gold, and find those nuggets worth polishing and including in your application.

EDITING

No word will be wasted--it's like leaving money on the table--and I'll guide you towards a final draft that is memorable, concise and within 5 words of the application's limit.

Helpful guidance on every step in the process from uncovering your talents and accomplishments through that polished draft where you put your best foot forward with polished prose.
